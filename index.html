Third Period - PE, Art, & Music

  • Third period - Specials - PE, Art, & Music

    There are three specials, Pe, Art, & Music. There are also 5 groups in which all of 5th grade is divided. These are A, B, C, D, & E. Note that specials can be in first, third, & sixth periods so there are 15 groups. I have it in third. On Monday, the A, B, & C groups will have PE, D has Art, & E has Music. On Tuesday, the A, D, & E groups will have PE, B has Art, & C has Music. On Wednesday, the B, C, & E groups will have PE, A has Art, & D has Music. On Thursday, the B, D, & E groups will have PE, C has Art, & A has Music. And finally, On Friday, the A, D, & C groups will have PE, E has Art, & B has Music. I will be discussing about PE, Art, & Music in the dropdowns below.

PE

GT

ELA

My fifth & sixth periods in school are ELA. My ELA teacher's name is Mrs. Broughton. ELA in OIS is very funny & exciting. We got to create stuff for special projects. We also had a lot of wordy & corny puzzles in computers which made our school day continue with a smart mind.

Seventh & Eighth Period - Mathematics
Math

My seventh & eighth periods in school are mathematics. My mathematics teacher's name in Mr. Anderson. Mathematics in OIS is very fun. We got to do a lot a stuff like being the teacher and teaching somebody one of the topics that we learned. One of the exciting things is the Harry Potter competition. All of 5th grade was divided into 4 groups which were Slytherin, Gryffindor, Ravenclaw, & Hufflepuff. I was in team Slytherin. In the whole competition, Slytherin & Gryffindor were in a fierce battle. Fortunately, I caught the golden snitch which gave us a 1000 points and Team Slytherin won. We also stopped Ravenclaw's winning streak of 3 wins in a row. This was at the end of the year in April, 2023 to May, 2023. This was hosted mainly for the STAAR test practice & if you don't know what the STAAR test is, you will know now. The STAAR test is the final test of the year which starts from 3rd grade and goes on until 12th grade. The STAAR test is hosted in Texas, USA.

    lunch & recess

  • Lunch & Recess

    Lunch is a very fun part of the schedule. You can eat lunch inside or outside the school which makes it even special. Also, in inside lunch, after 15 minutes, you can take out your phones. Now, if you want to have recess, after 15 minutes of lunch, outside or inside, you can go play. Recess is very well planned in OIS. There are 2 basketball hoops for knock-out, and 4 soccer goals. There is also a big space where we can do anything we want. That empty space is mainly used for running activities such as tag, freeze tag, infection, etc. This is what recess actually is.
